Our Story

Authentic Thai flavours, served with zero pretensions.

Nahm Pla is the Thai word for fish sauce. It's one of the most important ingredients in Thai cooking and the secret behind that bold, savory depth that makes Thai food so good.

At Nahm Pla Thai Dishes, we bring that same spirit to every dish. We cook with fresh ingredients and our own fish sauce blend to create flavors that are rich, balanced, and full of character.

Whether you're in the mood for a sizzling stir-fry, a bowl of noodles, a fragrant curry, or a fresh salad, every plate is made with care and packed with authentic Thai taste.
