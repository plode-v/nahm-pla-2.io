Our Story

Authentic Thai flavours, served with zero pretensions.

Nahm Pla — meaning "fish sauce" in Thai — is the humble, essential soul of every great Thai dish. That's exactly who we are: a neighbourhood restaurant grounded in the real flavours of Thailand, made fresh every day in our Baltimore kitchen.

We bring the bold curries, bright salads, and fragrant noodle soups that Thai families have shared for generations. Spice, freshness, and the flavours we grew up loving — served simply, honestly, and with heart.
